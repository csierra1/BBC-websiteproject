Coronavirus in the world

The global coronavirus cases passed 100 million cases

In the world, there are more than 102.9 million cases confirmed and 2.2 million of deaths across nearly 200 countries.

The countries with the highest number of confirmed cases are the US, India and Brazil and they are closely followed by a number of European countries. But the virus has surged across the world and very few places have managed to avoid it.

In Europe, the countries more affected by number of cases are:UK, Italy, France, Spain and Germany. By death rate: Belgium, Slovenia, UK, Chezch Republic and Italy.

Looking at the vaccinations, there are 61 countries with public data of vaccinations, 45 high income nations and 16 middle-income nations.
Israel, United Arab Emirates, UK, US, Italy and Germany are the countries with the highest number of dosses administred per 100 people.
