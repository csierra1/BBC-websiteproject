Which mask is better to wear?

All over the world, people are covering now their faces in million of ways

A homemade face coveringis the simpleast, cheapest promoted in 2020 because of worries about shortages of professional masks that medics desperately need.FFor example, made from an old t-shirt. Should do a reasonable job of stopping any virus that you might release.

Wearing a mask is very important as up to one in three people may be infected without even realising.

Can wear two masks, as you have more layers to stop the virus going through. Although, they are not recommended by the US government or the WHO.

Then, surgical masks. These are water resistant, so if someone coughs right at you the virus should be blocked. They provide good protection to you and others around you. But, they don´t fit very well. There are gaps in the sides that tiny virus particles called aerosols could get through

So, the best masks are FFP2, FFP3/NP95. They have filters inside to screen out those aerosols.In some countries, as Austria, they´re now required in buses and public areas. These have a much closer fit. No gaps, which means that’s which means you should be safer but they are a lot more expensive.A homemade face can be almost free, a surgical mask will cost about a euro and the FFP2, FFP3 around three euros.

The masks are not going to solve the pandemic themselves, but every action to reduce the impact is relevant.
